Select Tool

Replaces the structure block with a simple entity ui.
Move, rotate, flip, and undo all your mistakes.

1. Select

With the Selector hold down the <build/interact> button and release when done.

2. Resize

With the Selector hold down crouch and the <build/interact> button on an arrow release when done.

3. Move

With the Selector hold down the <build/interact> button on an arrow or center cube and release when done.

Extras

Open the Menu

First open the menu by pressing the <build/interact> button on a box you own.
Press the <build/interact> button to click a button.

4. Transform (Mirror & Rotate)

Press transform then pick a rotation or mirror.

5. Fill

Press fill then hit the input button with what blocks you want to add.


To choose how often a block occurs press the arrows, the inner arrows change the number by 1 and the outer arrows change the number by 10.

6. Duplicate

Press duplicate

7. Save As

Press Save As then input the name you want. This will give you a blueprint item of the build.
You can press <build/interact> to place the build on the ground, wall, or ceiling.

Other Items

Delete Item

Press <buld/interact> while looking at a selection to delete it. You can only delete unowned selections and your own selections.
Press Crouch and <buld/interact> to delete all selections that aren't owned

Undo Item

Press <buld/interact> to undo once.
Press Crouch and <buld/interact> to open up the global undo menu. From here you can review and multi-undo the actions of other players as well as delete the stored information for undo.

Tips & Tricks

Multi-Select

Press <buld/interact> to undo once.
Press Crouch and <buld/interact> to open up the global undo menu. From here you can review and multi-undo the actions of other players as well as delete the stored information for undo.
